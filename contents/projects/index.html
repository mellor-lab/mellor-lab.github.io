Our Projects#

We have a range of ongoing projects within our group spanning from the molecular to the systems level, with collaboration a key to our success. We utilise a range of methodologies including:

  • Brain slice electrophysiology

  • 2-photon imaging in brain slices

  • Behaviour in virtual reality with 2-photon imaging

  • Calcium imaging with miniaturised microscopes

  • Molecular biochemistry

  • Animal behaviour

  • Computational modelling
